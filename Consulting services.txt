Hyperautomation is a brand new trend that is taking 2020 by storm. This new trend has been ranked number one on Gartner's list of Top 10 Strategic Technology Trends for 2020.
Hyperautomation brings together several components of process automation, ranging from integrating tools to amplifying the ability to automate work.
It's main process starts with 'Robotic Process Automation (RPA)' at the very centre. It also brances into applying modern elements of 'Artificial Intelligence (AI)'.

Multiexperience is a thriving trend emergining in 2020. This trend has been ranked one of Gartners Top 10 Strategic Technology trends for 2020. 2020 has been marked as the year when the technology-literate people are going to be replaced by people-literate technology.
Multiexperience transforms the idea of single interaction with computers into a large scale multisensory and multitouchpoint technology
Valve and HTC's 'VIVE' is the first-of-its-kind virtual reality system which emerged onto the market in 2015 and has since captivated its audience
